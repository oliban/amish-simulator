Terrain Directory
=================

Purpose: Ground tiles, terrain textures, and field layouts.

Assets needed:
- pasture_ground: Green pasture ground texture/tile
- plowed_field: Dirt field texture for plowing mini-game
- path tiles: Walking paths between farm areas

Kenney packs to reference:
- Nature Kit: ground_grass, ground_pathStraight, ground_pathBend, etc.
  (Located at Art/kenney/kenney_nature-kit/Models/FBX format/ground_*.fbx)
- Nature Kit: crops_dirtRow, crops_dirtSingle, crops_dirtDoubleRow
  (Located at Art/kenney/kenney_nature-kit/Models/FBX format/crops_dirt*.fbx)
- Isometric Roads: Isometric path/road tiles
  (Located at Art/kenney/kenney_isometric-roads/png/)

License: All Kenney assets are CC0.

Notes:
- Ground tiles from Nature Kit are 3D modular pieces
- Dirt row models can create plowed field grids
- Seasonal color changes may need custom materials
