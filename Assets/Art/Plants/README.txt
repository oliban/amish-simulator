Plants Directory
================

Purpose: Crop models, trees, bushes, and other vegetation.

Assets needed:
- crop_wheat: Wheat crop at various growth stages
- crop_corn: Corn crop at various growth stages
- Trees: Various tree types for farm environment
- Bushes and flowers: Decorative vegetation

Kenney packs to reference:
- Nature Kit crops (Art/kenney/kenney_nature-kit/Models/FBX format/):
  - crops_wheatStageA.fbx, crops_wheatStageB.fbx
  - crops_cornStageA.fbx through crops_cornStageD.fbx
  - crops_leafsStageA.fbx, crops_leafsStageB.fbx
  - crop_carrot.fbx, crop_melon.fbx, crop_pumpkin.fbx, crop_turnip.fbx
- Nature Kit trees:
  - tree_default.fbx, tree_oak.fbx, tree_pine*.fbx
  - Seasonal variants: tree_*_fall.fbx, tree_*_dark.fbx
- Nature Kit plants:
  - plant_bush.fbx, plant_bushLarge.fbx, flower_*.fbx

License: All Kenney assets are CC0.

Notes:
- Corn has 4 growth stages (A-D), wheat has 2 (A-B)
- Fall tree variants can be used for autumn season
- SeasonSystem may swap tree materials per season
