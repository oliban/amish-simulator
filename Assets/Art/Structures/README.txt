Structures Directory
====================

Purpose: Buildings and structural elements (barn, houses, fences).

Assets needed:
- barn: Amish-style barn for barn-raising mini-game
- fence: Fence segments for farm boundaries
- house: Simple Amish farmhouse
- well: Water well prop

Kenney packs to reference:
- Nature Kit: fence_simple, fence_planks, fence_gate, fence_bend, fence_corner
  (Located at Art/kenney/kenney_nature-kit/Models/FBX format/fence_*.fbx)
- Fantasy Town Kit: Medieval buildings (needs manual download)
- Tiny Town: 2D building tiles as UI/minimap fallbacks

License: All Kenney assets are CC0. Custom barn model must be original.

Notes:
- The barn is central to the BarnRaising mini-game
- Fences from Nature Kit can be used directly
- Consider ProBuilder for simple barn geometry
