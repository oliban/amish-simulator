Audio Directory
===============

Purpose: Sound effects and music for the game.

Assets needed:
- Ambient farm sounds (birds, wind, animals)
- Tool interaction sounds (churning, plowing, chopping)
- UI click/select sounds
- Background music (folk/acoustic style)

Kenney packs available:
- No audio packs were downloaded in this batch
- Kenney has UI audio packs at https://kenney.nl/assets (search for "audio")

Other free audio sources:
- https://freesound.org (various CC licenses)
- https://opengameart.org (various CC licenses)
- Kenney UI Audio: https://kenney.nl/assets/ui-audio

License: Check individual licenses per source. Kenney audio is CC0.

Notes:
- Audio is lower priority than visual assets
- Unity's AudioSource can use WAV, MP3, or OGG formats
