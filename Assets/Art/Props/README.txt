Props Directory
===============

Purpose: Interactive objects, tools, and props for mini-games.

Assets needed:
- butter_churn: Butter churning prop for ButterChurning mini-game
- plow: Plow tool for Plowing mini-game
- axe: Wood chopping tool
- bucket: Water/milk carrying prop
- quilting_frame: Frame for QuiltingBee mini-game

Kenney packs to reference:
- Nature Kit: log.fbx, log_stack.fbx, campfire_*.fbx, canoe_paddle.fbx
  (Located at Art/kenney/kenney_nature-kit/Models/FBX format/)
- No direct matches for butter churn, plow, or axe in available packs

License: All Kenney assets are CC0. Custom tool models must be original.

Notes:
- Butter churn and plow are HIGH PRIORITY custom models
- Consider simple geometric shapes as placeholders (cylinder for churn, box+triangle for plow)
- Log and log_stack from Nature Kit can be used for wood chopping scenarios
